Case study

From manual KPI chasing to automated delivery

A reporting workflow designed to make recurring metrics arrive consistently, visibly, and with less manual effort.

Metabasen8nGoogle SheetsKPI AutomationData Delivery

Context

Recurring KPI reporting often looks simple from the outside, but repeated exports, formatting, validation, logging, and delivery consume analytical time and create hidden operational risk.

The challenge

Manual handoffs delay updates, weaken traceability, and make the process dependent on one person remembering every step.

My role

I designed the workflow as a small internal data product: a controlled metric source, a visible trace where needed, an automation layer, and a clear delivery destination.

What I changed

  • Structured the required metrics in Metabase.
  • Used Google Sheets as a visible logging or staging layer where appropriate.
  • Connected the workflow through n8n for scheduled movement and delivery.
  • Added checks to reduce the risk of sending incomplete or misleading updates.
  • Designed the output for the channel where stakeholders already work.
Lessmanual follow-up
Moretraceability
Fasterrecurring delivery

The main lesson

Good automation does not merely save clicks. It makes the reporting process observable, repeatable, and easier to trust.

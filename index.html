05 / Who is Hani Ashour?

A hands-on analyst with a data-manager mindset.

Hani Ashour is a Palestinian data management and business intelligence professional with a software engineering background and hands-on experience across digital products, product teams, operations, marketing, and management.

His work focuses on trusted metrics, data quality, reporting automation, and turning complex business questions into clear, actionable analytical systems.

What is his current role?

Data Manager & Business Intelligence at Darent.

What are his core areas?

Data management, business intelligence, product analytics, data quality, KPI governance, SQL, dashboards, and automation.

Where does he work?

Based in Palestine and open to remote work and collaboration with regional and international teams.

Is Hani a data analyst or a data manager?

He currently works as a Data Manager & Business Intelligence professional while remaining hands-on with SQL, analysis, dashboards, and data investigations.

What types of problems does he solve?

Misaligned KPI definitions, data-quality issues, manual reporting, non-actionable dashboards, and translating departmental needs into clear measurement systems.

01 / HOW I THINK

A dashboard is not the product.

The real product is the decision someone can make because the data is finally clear, trusted, and available at the right moment.

01

Start with the business question

Before SQL, charts, or automation, I define what the team is actually trying to understand or change.

02

Make the metric trustworthy

Definitions, source alignment, validation, and ownership come before celebrating a number.

03

Design for action

The output should make the next step obvious — not create another meeting to explain the dashboard.
